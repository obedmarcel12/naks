    ABOUT NAKS HOTEL & TOWERS

    Founded with a vision to blend world-class hospitality with authentic
    Heritage Touch, Naks Hotel has grown into a premier destination for
    travelers from around the globe. Naks Hotel is a 3-star Hotel located
    at the heart of Calabar.

    It has one hundred and three (103) functional
    rooms, two halls of different capacities, and 24-hour internet access.
    It also has a well-equipped kitchen managed by experienced caterers
    for continental and national dishes. Our security system is of
    international standard with well-trained personnel for your maximum
    safety. Naks Hotels has recreational facilities like a squash court,
    standard swimming pool, gym, and other facilities for guests’ use
    without extra charge.

    Experience a classy ambience, modern, graceful,
    extremely spacious, comfortable and international standard rooms
    particularly set to make you feel at home during your stay. Facilities
    that make you relax and even work while you stay (full satellite
    multi-channels, internet connections). At Naks Hotels & Towers, you
    will experience mouthwatering dishes – à la carte, buffet services,
    exotic bars with associated drinks, garden bar with barbecue centre
    and live music entertainment.
