    • ABOUT NAKS HOTEL & TOWERS

    Founded with a vision to blend world-class hospitality with authentic Heritage Touch, Naks Hotel has grown into a premier destination for travelers from around the globe.

    Naks Hotel is a 3'star Hotel located at the heart of calabar. It has one Hundred and three (103) functional Rooms, Two Halls of different capacities and 24 hours internet access. It also has well equipped kitchen managed by experienced carterers for continental and national dishes. our security system is of internaional standard with well trained security personnel for your maximum security.

    Naks Hotels has recreational facilities like Squash court, standard Swimming Pool, Gym and Other facilities for guests use without extra charge.
    Experience a Classy Ambience, Modern, Graceful, Extremely Spacious, Comfortable and International standard rooms particulary set to make you at home during your stay. facilities that make you relax and even work while you stay (full Satelite Multi-Channels, Internet Connections).
